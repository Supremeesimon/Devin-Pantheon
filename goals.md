# Devin Pantheon Goals

## Primary Goal
Automate the discovery and outreach of private car sellers in Lethbridge, AB, to facilitate brokerage deals with local dealerships.

## Objectives
- **Scrape Kijiji Listings:** Identify "For Sale By Owner" car listings in Lethbridge.
- **Extract Contact Info:** Prioritize ads with phone numbers in the description.
- **Automate Outreach:** Trigger Synthflow calls via the "Sarah" persona.
- **Maintain Persistent Memory:** Log all interactions and states to GitHub.
- **Scale Operations:** Achieve a consistent flow of closed deals and finder's fees.
